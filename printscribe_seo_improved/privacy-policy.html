PrintScribe PH ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website or purchase any of our products and services.

Non-Personal Information:

We use your information to:

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill your order, comply with legal obligations, and for internal reporting purposes.

7. Your Rights

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccuracies

You can contact us to exercise these rights via email.
